About Us

At Service and Sales, we're here to keep your home running smoothly. Serving Herts and North London, we specialise in repairing all types of domestic appliances - from fridges and washing machines to ovens and dishwashers.

With over 50 years of experience and a passion for quality service, we're commited to fast, realible, and affordable repairs. Our friendly team understands how important your appliances are to daily life, which is why we focus on clear communication, honest advice, and lasting solutions.

Whether it's a quick fix or a more complex repair, you can count on Services and Sales to get the job done right - first time, every time.
